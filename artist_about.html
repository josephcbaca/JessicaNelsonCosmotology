It's entirely possible to remain inspired by the universe surrounding and to optimistically traverse the negative space, by accepting the chaos and letting it all happen against fight and will. Accept the insignificance as a strength to guide the ideology that nothing will be changed by the smallness of this existance. Then, build upon the nothingness to form gratitude, love, truths, absolutes and infinite wisdom and prepare for all of it to come crashing down at any instance.


Joseph Baca is from Placentia, California and currently resides in Seattle, Washington.
