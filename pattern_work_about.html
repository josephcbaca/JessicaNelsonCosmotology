The Pattern Work series tempts subject bias. Through basic lines and shapes, each piece forms a harmonious habitat for each element to live within the negative space of lots color. This series could either be considered easily accessible and palatable by the viewer or out rejected. My intention is happiness. I'm looking to make you smile, but again, don't take my word for it.
